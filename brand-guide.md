# 同道 Bible Brand Starter Kit

## Name
**同道 Bible**  
Pinyin: **tóng dào Bible**  
Chinese version: **同道聖經**  
Tagline: **Scripture Together.**

## Suggested colours
- Ink: `#24211E`
- Warm Gold: `#B79A68`
- Soft Cream: `#FBFAF7`
- Paper White: `#FFFFFF`
- Mist Border: `#E8E0D6`
- Muted Text: `#716960`
- Deep Navy: `#111C26`

## Suggested type direction
- Chinese display: Noto Serif CJK TC, Songti TC, Source Han Serif TC, or custom calligraphy.
- English UI: Inter, SF Pro, system UI, or Noto Sans.
- Reading text: Source Serif, Noto Serif, Georgia, or another highly readable serif.

## Logo direction
This starter pack uses a refined serif placeholder. For the real final logo, use this as art direction and have a calligrapher create a lighter version of Option C/E:
- elegant,
- minimal,
- not too thick,
- readable at app-icon size,
- scalable as SVG/vector.
